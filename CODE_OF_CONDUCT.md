# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and evidence-focused community.
Participation must be free from harassment and discrimination, regardless of
background, identity, career stage, institution, disability, or level of
technical experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Critique ideas, code, data, and evidence rather than people.
- Acknowledge uncertainty, correct mistakes transparently, and give credit.
- Protect confidential, personal, licensed, and embargoed information.
- Respect project scope, scientific-review decisions, and contributor time.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, sexualized attention,
threats, deliberate disclosure of private information, repeated disruption,
and retaliation are not acceptable in project spaces or when representing the
project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `xuexin@njucm.edu.cn`. Do not include
more personal or sensitive information than is necessary. Maintainers will
review reports confidentially, avoid conflicts of interest, and may remove
content, limit participation, or take other proportionate action. A person
named in a report will not adjudicate that report.

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards. Good-faith reports and participation in a review must not result in
retaliation.
